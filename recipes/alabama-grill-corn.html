Alabama Grill Corn

"Alabama Grill Corn" is my version of Mexican Street Corn, or at least that's where I got the idea from. It's a savory, stick-in-your-teeth kinda dish that goes perfectly with barbeque.

For this one, you're gonna want to follow the recipe here. You'll also have to make the choice bewtween blue cheese or feta cheese -- I know, blue isn't to everyone's tastes, but if you like it it's exceptional on this dish.

Ingredients

  • Corn on the Cob
  • Alabama White Sauce
  • Blue Cheese or Feta Cheese
  • Aluminum Foil

Steps

  1. Grill the corn, allowing certain areas to brown to your liking
  2. When the corn is cooked, put it in the aluminum foil
  3. Top the corn with the Alabama White Sauce and your choice of cheese
  4. Close the foil and allow to steam
  5. Remove from foil after five minutes or when ready to serve and enjoy!
