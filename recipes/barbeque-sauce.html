Alabama White Sauce

Description

This sauce is tangy in the best of ways.It's mayonnaise base makes it easily slatherable while the apple cider vinegar and horseradish add a subtle warmth.

You can put this on chicken, beef, or pork!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon prepared horseradish
  • 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion poweder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper (or to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional for heat)
  • salt to taste
  • a very small pinch of freshly ground nutmeg
  • a splash of water if needed for consistency

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, adding nutmeg last and adjusting to taste
  2. Whisk together ingredients into cohesive sauce.
  3. Add water if needed to adjust the consistency to your desired viscosity
  4. Refrigerate for at least an hour
  5. Serve and enjoy!
